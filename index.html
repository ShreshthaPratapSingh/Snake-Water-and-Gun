Rules:
So the rules here are simple. You need to make a choice between snake(🐍) | Water(💧) | Gun(🔫). The system will also choose one of the above. The system and you will be playing a total of 10 rounds after which the winner will be decided. The winner of each round as per the choice will be decided as follows:
  • Snake drinks Water → Snake wins
  • Water douses Gun → Water wins
  • Gun kills Snake → Gun wins
  • Same choice → It's a draw
